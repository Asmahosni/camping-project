Finding and Setting Up a Campsite

Many people like to head to the great outdoors for vacations or quick getaways. But spending a few days (and nights) in a tent or a camper can be daunting for first-timers. Don't worry. Camping gets easier every time, and before you know it you'll be setting up your tent in a matter of seconds and preparing gourmet meals on a camp stove.
